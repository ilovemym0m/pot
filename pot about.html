About Peace of the Mind!

For the sanity of humanity.

Business Type: We Are a Service

Mission Statement

Our mission is to give the agitated a chance at rehabilitation, to give affordable prices to people in need, and to show others that we can make a difference to assist people with struggles with being calm.

Where/How are we selling?

We will sell our services at M.A.C.A.D.E.M.Y School Of Science And Technology for the students, to bring serenity, tranquility, and composure to the young minds.

Why are we starting this business?

We are starting this business in our school, because there are people who are agitated from difficulties in understanding their work given, having afflictions and or stressed from having a distressing day, etc. We are benefiting the students with our business provided to help comfort them when they are agitated, some of these circumstances developed in young minds, especially in the black communities due to the disparities of the Covid-19 pandemic, causing antisocial activities, anxiety, and lackadaisical behaviors.

Who are we starting this business for?

This business is for children, and we hope to help them by using various calming methods. Due to the number of people around the world that face mental challenges, our company will allow others to pay cheap affordable prices so they can get the serenity they need.
